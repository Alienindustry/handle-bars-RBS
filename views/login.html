Forget Me Not.

So you’ve been shopping and spent your hard earned cash on your weekly shop, and like us you hardly think about their sell by dates once they're put away in your fridge, freezer, pantry and so the food is wasted, and your money too, and what do we all do next we simply chuck them away …well Forget Me Not is here to help, with just a few simple clicks you can organise your eating habits and help fight against food waste.

Forget Me Not will tell you when and what food you have to eat, and best of all before it’s out of date, hopefully saveing your money and the environment, click ENTER to create your account.
